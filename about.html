Wushu

I started wushu (Chinese martial arts) when I was 8 most likely due to my mother's wishes to disperse some of my energy elsewhere. Since then, wushu has been not only an outlet for stress, but it has also given me a community of supportive and hilarious friends. I'm currently an active member of Cal Wushu, where I've made many of my favorite memories and closest friends.

Traveling

I love exploring new places. One of my favorite adventures was backpacking through Plumas National Forest, where our surroundings actually matched the pictures on the packaging of our freeze dried dinner. Even though my current college-student definition of traveling mainly involves visiting nearby parks, I'm always eager to spend the day outside. I'm particularly obsessed with beach trips and finding nice places to watch the sunset.
